about the academy

At Claro Academy, tennis is more than a sport—it is a passion that shapes character, builds resilience, and brings community together. Located in a pristine valley, we provide state-of-the-art facilities and a customized coaching syllabus that adapts to individual player goals.

Our staff consists of former touring professionals and ITF-certified coaches who are dedicated to unlocking your potential. With structured learning systems and regular match play, we ensure that every player leaves the court stronger, faster, and more confident than when they walked on.
